Return Policy

Trupar Forklift Parts is committed to your satisfaction. We accept returns within 30 days of the purchase date on most items.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be unused and in original, uninstalled condition
  • Items must be returned in original packaging
  • Proof of purchase is required
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of the delivery date

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, contact our customer service team:

  • Phone: 1-800-222-9606
  • Website: Contact Us

Once your return is approved, ship the item back to our warehouse using a trackable shipping method. We will process your refund within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item.

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Electrical components (solenoids, sensors, switches) once installed
  • Special-order or custom parts
  • Items showing signs of installation, use, or damage

Defective or Incorrect Items

If you received a defective or incorrect item, please contact us within 30 days of delivery. We will cover return shipping and send a replacement or issue a full refund at no additional cost.
